Animating Music Videos In Europe!

Our five member 3D team produced fully animated scenes and added special effects to live action for music videos.

It was honestly organized chaos. Little bits and pieces of animation were requested weekly. Each request got pinned to a cork board, we'd then sketch out the keyframes, develop a visual style for approval, and produce each clip within 5-10 work days. It was a lot of fun.
